Proposal

Enrich your collector

Culture Curious is a white-label cultural intelligence layer that sits alongside your Preferred programme. Collectors use it to plan their cultural season. Sotheby's gains a real-time map of where their clients are, what they care about, and when to activate.

The Starting Point
What Sotheby's already has
A Preferred programme — a curated club of collectors who receive exclusive access to studios, museum tours, private previews. A relationship asset built over decades.
The gap today
Collector profiles capture what they buy — not what they love. Who is going to Vienna Philharmonic next month? Who will be in Paris for the opera season? That data doesn't exist yet.
What becomes possible
When you know a collector is attending Netrebko at ROH on Friday, you can place a Sotheby's dinner in their calendar for Thursday evening — elegantly, without cold outreach.
How It Works — The Client Journey
01
Collector opts in via Preferred+
A white-label cultural profile form — branded as Sotheby's — is sent to Preferred members. They input favourite performers, composers, venues, and cultural cities. Feels like a luxury benefit, not a survey.
Sotheby's branded
02
App surfaces their season — across all cities
Culture Curious syncs the global cultural calendar and shows each collector their relevant events: opera in Vienna, ballet in Paris, piano recital in Amsterdam — all in one place. The collector books what interests them.
Value to the collector
03
Sotheby's dashboard sees the cultural map
The Preferred programme dashboard gets a live view: which collectors are going where, when, and what they care about. Cultural preference enriches every existing CRM record — silently, automatically.
CRM enrichment
04
Sotheby's activates around the moment
A collector is attending a Tacita Dean–designed production at ROH → Sotheby's places a private studio visit or collector dinner in the same week. No cold outreach — the timing is already perfect.
Intelligent activation
05
Relationship deepens — naturally
The collector experiences Sotheby's as deeply attuned to their world. Loyalty strengthens. Their +1s become new contacts in the Preferred pipeline. The collection grows alongside the relationship.
Loyalty + pipeline
