System Architecture: Four-Agent Consortium

Patient inputs: blood tests, DNA, wearables, nutrition diary, symptoms

Agent What It Does Output
Clinical Geneticist Analyzes DNA. Which genes are variants? What do they mean for health? Genetic report + predispositions
Pharmacogenomicist Cross-references meds + genes. Will this drug work? Is it safe? Drug-gene interaction warnings
Nutritionist Analyzes diet + genes. Which foods/supplements help? Personalized nutrition plan
Biostatistician Validates outputs. Are they backed by enough evidence? Confidence score + flags weak claims
Doctor receives: One-page brief with patient's genetic risks, medication warnings, nutrition blind spots, biological age. All backed by peer-reviewed sources.
